Iced Caramel Latte

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Espresso
  • Caramel syrup
  • Caramel sauce
  • Vanilla syrup
  • Full Cream Milk

Direction

  1. In a glass, put ice.
  2. Espresso combine with caramel syrup and vanilla syrup. Mix them together.
  3. Pour the milk in the glass.
  4. Lastly, pour the espresso.
  5. Drizzle a couple line of frost and caramel sauce on top, if you want.
  6. Enjoy!
Good Luck!
